Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

What is Kadam?

Kadam is a freelance platform connecting students with employers for project-based work. We aim to provide students with valuable experience and businesses with access to emerging talent.

How do I sign up?

You can sign up as a student or an employer through our signup page. The process is quick and easy!

Is there a fee to use Kadam?

It's free to sign up and browse jobs or talent. Kadam takes a small percentage of the transaction as a platform fee once a project is completed to maintain and improve our services.

How do I get paid as a student?

Payments are handled securely through our platform. Once you complete a project and the employer approves it, the funds are transferred to your Kadam account, which you can then withdraw.

What kind of projects can I find on Kadam?

You can find a wide range of projects, from web and graphic design to software development, writing, and video production. There are opportunities for students of all skill levels.

How do you ensure the quality of students?

We have a verification process for students to confirm their identity and skills. Employers can also view student portfolios and reviews to assess their qualifications before hiring.
