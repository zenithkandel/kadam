## üîé Overview

<div align="left" style="font-size: 1.05rem; line-height: 1.75;">

Here is a brief description of the pages of the site:

- **Landing page**
  - Navigation bar
    - (Home, About, Contact, FAQ)
    - Login/Signup **OR** User -> Dashboard
  - Hero section
  - About
  - Testimonial
  - Contact
  - FAQ
  - Footer
- **Signup**
  - Student
    - Name
    - Email
    - Phone
    - Username (can't be changed)  
            (autogenerated using _<https://randomuser.me/api/>_)
    - Address
  - Employer
    - Name
    - Email
    - Phone
    - Address
    - Are you a company or an individual? (Company | Individual)
- **Account verification**
  - Student
    - Email verification
    - Phone number verification
    - Profile picture with clear face (eyes nose ears)
    - Selection of abilities and expertise from the list as well as customs. (max 15)
    - evidence of the selected expertise (photo, video, link, certificate, awards, websites, social profiles, etc....)
    - Curriculum Vitae (C.V.)
    - Age for legal purposes
    - Dated ID card for age verification (ID card/ Citizenship/ Passport/ Driving License)
    - If aged below 18 then guardian's approval signed letter and guardian's contact number
    - Agreement to terms and conditions
    - (Note: the verification process takes 2-3 business days. Please upload valid and real documents for verification.)
  - Employer
    - Company
      - Company Name
      - Logo
      - Email verification
      - Phone verification
      - Signed and stamped letter of application for registration in the site in the company letterhead
      - Executive phone number
      - Technical phone number
      - (Note: the verification process takes 2-3 business days. Please upload valid and real documents for verification.)
    - Individual
      - Email verification
      - Phone number verification
      - Profile picture with clear face (eyes nose ears)
      - ID card for verification (Citizenship/ Passport/ Driving License/ National ID Card)
      - Manually signed agreement to the terms and conditions.
      - Manually signed registration application letter.
      - Digital agreement to terms and conditions.
      - (Note: the verification process takes 2-3 business days. Please upload valid and real documents for verification.)
- **Profile Management Dashboard:**
  - Student
    - Name
    - Profile picture (needs verification for face existence)
    - Contact information
    - Social Media
    - Bio (20 words)
    - About Paragraphs (200 words)
    - Skills listing:  
            \- Users can list out their skills in bullet points up to 15 skills per person. For example, "I can make websites. I can design logos. I can make animated videos"
    - Level Details  
            \- Users collect experience points for each successfully completed task. Gathering experience points helps them level up and show their experience in the field
    - Work demos  
            \- Users can upload the work samples that they have been doing to prove their skills. They can upload images, attach links or videos as samples of their experience.
    - Tags Management:  
            \- Users can assign 20 tags to their account so that when anyone places a query on the platform, their account gets indexed based on the search tags.
  - Employer
    - Individual
      - Name
      - Profile picture (needs verification for face existence)
      - Contact details
      - Social Media
      - Bio (20 words)
      - About (200 words)
      - Level Details  
                \- Users collect experience points for each successfully completed task. Gathering experience points helps them level up and show their trust in the field.
      - Search Tags  
                \- Users can assign 20 tags to their account so that when anyone places a query on the platform, their account gets indexed based on the search tags.
      - Active Vacancies / Task offers
    - Company
      - Name (needs verification for validity)
      - Profile picture
      - Contact details
      - Social Media
      - Bio (20 words)
      - About (200 words)
      - Level Details  
                \- Companies collect experience points for each successfully completed task. Gathering experience points helps them level up and show their trust in the field.
      - Search Tags  
                \- Companies can assign 20 tags to their account so that when anyone places a query on the platform, their account gets indexed based on the search tags.
      - Active Vacancies / Task offers
- **Task uploading parameters:**
  - Company/Individual Employer
    - Task:
      - Task title (15 words)
      - Detailed Task Description (200 words)
      - Task Category (Website, Software, Computer Hardware, Art, Music, Decoration, Custom Jewelry, Graphics Design, Logo Making, Thumbnail Making, Advertisement, Videography, English Literature, Nepali literature, Song writing, Other)
      - Number of workers required
      - Estimated time required
      - Net amount  
                \-Minimum net amount is automatically calculated depending on the estimated time required. (1 hour = 100rs minimum)  
                \- **_Kadam_** takes 8% of your offer as platform fees.  
                \- You can easily calculate within the platform about the fees for the platform as well as the student. (Student's Income= Your offer - platform fees)
      - Related Image  
                \- An image which represents your work the best. If no such image is available, the platform automatically assigns a placeholder image depending on the task category.  
                \- If the category is not specified (others) then a default placeholder will be placed.
      - Contact Information
      - Deadline for applications
- **Students Job Seeking/Doing Mechanism:  
    \-** Students can search for jobs from the explore section from their dashboard.  
    \- Students can search for listed jobs and filter as per their choice.  
    \- A recommendation feed will be presented to them as per their skills and preferences  
    \- Students can view the details for listed jobs.  
    \- Students also can be directly requested to do tasks by the employers. And in that case, the students will be asked to accept or reject the request and if accepted, the task will be added to the inventory and will continue as normal.  
    \- Students can apply for the listed jobs with their offering price which will also send their CV as well as their **_Kadam_** profile to the employer.  
    \-Students can see their applications to the jobs that they have applied and can remove their applications any time before acceptance.  
    \- Students will be notified when they get accepted by an employer and then the task will be added to the student's inventory.  
    \- After getting accepted, the employer and the student will have a talk and confirm to start the work along with the final deal price mentioned in the platform.  
    \- A digital agreement will be signed by both parties and the 10% advance payment is sent to the student.  
    \- The student must complete the work on the mentioned deadline and if unable to do so, they can inform the employer to increase the deadline.  
    \- Being unable to complete the task in deadline will result in negative experience which will decrease the level of the student and if repeated will result in a ban from the platform and possible legal consequences will be faced.  
    \- Upon completion of the task, the task can be sent via the platform itself. The platform supports (image, video, audio, archives, etc.) up to 1GB per file size limit  
    \- After submitting the related task and marking the task completed, the student will get experience points based on the work hours assigned for the job.  
    \- Finally, after receiving the payment, the student will mark as payment received and the task will be closed and archived.  
    \- Both the student and the employer can rate each other for betterment of the application.
- **Employer Task posting/assigning mechanism:  
    \-** The employer will assign all the parameters as mentioned in **_5(Task uploading parameters)_**  
    \- The employer will then select whether to assign the task directly to the selected candidate or to post on their profile for the candidates to browse themselves and find them out.  
    \- If the task is requested directly to a candidate then the employer will need to wait for the candidate to accept their task.  
    \- If the task is posted in their profile, the employer can see the applications and the applicant's profile to analyze the best possible candidate and assign them the task.  
    \- Then, after accepting from both parties, another digital agreement is signed and the task will be started by the candidate after 10% advance payment from the employer.  
    \- Finally, after all the works is done and received, the employer sends the remaining 90% of the cost.  

-- **Admin Panel:**
  - Login System:  
        \- Admin Users will manually be defined in the code so that no security breach is possible.  
        \- Logs will be made for every login
  - Main Dashboard:
    - **Home Stats Page**
      - Total active employers
      - Total active students
      - Total daily views
      - Total ongoing tasks
      - Total completed tasks
      - Total pending tasks
      - Total verification requests
      - Total unverified employers
      - Total unverified students
      - Total admin accounts
      - Admin logs (sortable and downloadable)
      - Report requests
      - Total revenue generated (platform fees collected)
      - Active support tickets
      - Recent logins and activities
    - **Verification Management**
      - View, verify, or reject student and employer verification requests.
      - Inspect uploaded documents and mark verification as pending, approved, or denied with remarks.
      - Send automated emails regarding verification status.
      - Maintain a history log of all verifications for transparency and audit purposes.
    - **User Management**
      - View, search, edit, suspend, or delete any student or employer account.
      - Reset user passwords and manage access levels.
      - Filter users based on verification status, activity, or report counts.
      - Assign "trusted" or "flagged" labels to specific users.
      - Export user data in CSV format for records or analysis.
    - **Task Management**
      - View and manage all posted tasks (active, ongoing, completed, or reported).
      - Force-close or freeze tasks in case of disputes or violations.
      - Analyze task statistics (category-wise, time-based, or income-based).
      - Approve or deny reported tasks after investigation.
    - **Reports & Violations**
      - View all reported users or tasks submitted by community members.
      - Investigate reports with evidence (screenshots, chat logs, etc.).
      - Take appropriate actions (warnings, suspension, ban).
      - Maintain a strike count system for users.
      - Generate weekly or monthly violation summaries.
    - **Payment Management**
      - Monitor all payment transactions and platform fees.
      - View pending, completed, and disputed payments.
      - Handle refund or compensation requests manually.
      - Export payment logs for accounting and compliance.
    - **Communication & Notifications**
      - Send system-wide announcements, notifications, or updates to users.
      - Manage FAQs and automated message templates.
      - Broadcast urgent alerts (e.g., downtime, maintenance, new policy).
    - **Analytics & Insights**
      - View graphical charts of user growth, task trends, revenue, and engagement.
      - Filter analytics by date range, region, or category.
      - Export charts and statistics for administrative reports.
    - **Security & Logs**
      - View and clear detailed admin activity logs.
      - Monitor failed login attempts, suspicious IPs, or abnormal user behaviors.
      - Enable/disable security features (2FA, session limits, password policies).
      - Backup and restore system data securely.
    - **System Settings**
      - Manage platform configurations such as fee percentage, minimum rates, deadlines, and storage limits.
      - Manage content (FAQs, Terms & Conditions, Privacy Policy).
      - Update logos, contact info, or global site details.
      - Create, edit, or delete admin roles with tiered access (e.g., Super Admin, Moderator, Verifier, Accountant).
