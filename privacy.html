Important: This Privacy Policy explains how KADAM collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information, including sensitive verification documents, ID cards, payment information, and task-related data. By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy, including mandatory verification, identity checks, and information sharing necessary for the student-employer matching process.

1. Introduction

Welcome to KADAM's Privacy Policy. KADAM is a platform designed to connect skilled Students with Employers (companies or individuals) who need tasks completed. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information throughout the verification process, task matching, and payment transactions.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform, including our website and mobile applications (collectively, the "Services"). Given the nature of our platform—which requires identity verification, skill validation, and financial transactions—we collect and process various types of personal information to ensure trust and safety for all users.

We understand that you trust us with your personal information, and we take that responsibility seriously. This policy is designed to help you understand:

  • What information we collect during registration, verification, and platform usage
  • How we use that information to match students with employers and facilitate task completion
  • How we protect your sensitive documents and financial information
  • The choices we offer, including how to access and update information
  • The measures we take to keep your information secure

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the Services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information in connection with the Services we provide, including:

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

Account Registration Information:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Username (auto-generated and permanent for students)
  • Password
  • Physical address
  • Account type selection (Student, Individual Employer, or Company Employer)

Verification Documents (Mandatory):

As part of our mandatory verification process (2-3 business days), we collect:

For Students:

  • Profile picture with clear facial features (eyes, nose, ears visible)
  • Government-issued ID card (ID card, Citizenship, Passport, or Driving License) with date
  • Age verification information
  • Guardian approval letter and contact information (if under 18 years)
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Skills and expertise selections (up to 15 skills)
  • Evidence of expertise (certificates, awards, portfolio links, work samples)
  • Signed agreement to Terms and Conditions

For Individual Employers:

  • Profile picture with clear facial features
  • Valid ID card (Citizenship, Passport, Driving License, or National ID)
  • Manually signed Terms and Conditions agreement
  • Manually signed registration application letter

For Company Employers:

  • Company name and logo
  • Signed and stamped application letter on company letterhead
  • Executive phone number
  • Technical phone number

Profile Information:

  • Bio (maximum 20 words)
  • About section (maximum 200 words)
  • Social media links
  • Skills listing (students: up to 15 skills)
  • Work samples and portfolio (images, videos, links)
  • Search tags (up to 20 tags)

Task and Transaction Information:

  • Task postings (title, description, category, requirements, deadlines)
  • Task applications and proposals
  • Estimated work hours and payment amounts
  • Digital agreements between students and employers
  • Task deliverables (files up to 1GB: images, videos, audio, archives)
  • Payment information (advance 10% and final 90% payments)
  • Experience points and level status

Communication Data:

  • Messages between students and employers through the platform
  • Customer support inquiries and tickets
  • Survey responses and feedback
  • Ratings and reviews
  • Dispute reports and evidence

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you access our Services, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage:

Device Information:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Device identifiers
  • Screen resolution and display settings

Usage Information:

  • Pages viewed and time spent on pages
  • Links clicked and search queries
  • Access times and dates
  • Referring and exit pages
  • Application interactions and features used

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third-party sources, including:

  • Social media platforms (if you connect your account)
  • Background check providers and identity verification services (as part of mandatory verification)
  • Analytics providers
  • Payment processors and financial institutions
  • Government ID verification systems
  • Educational institutions (for student verification, with consent)
  • Publicly available sources

Why We Collect Extensive Verification Data

KADAM requires comprehensive verification to ensure trust and safety. Since we connect students (including minors with guardian consent) with employers for paid work, we must verify:

  • Identity authenticity: To prevent fraud and ensure genuine users
  • Age verification: To comply with labor laws and protect minors
  • Skills validation: To ensure students can deliver promised work
  • Employer legitimacy: To protect students from fraudulent job postings
  • Payment reliability: To facilitate secure financial transactions

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on:

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 With Other Users

Visible to Other Users: When you create a profile on KADAM, certain information is visible to other users:

  • For Students: Name, profile picture, bio, about section, skills list, work samples/portfolio, experience level, ratings/reviews, and search tags are visible to employers browsing the platform or reviewing your application
  • For Employers: Name/company name, logo, bio, about section, experience level, active task postings, ratings/reviews, and search tags are visible to students

Task Applications: When you apply for a task, your CV, profile, and application details are shared with the employer. When you're directly requested for a task, the employer can view your complete profile.

During Task Execution: Contact information, task agreements, deliverables, and communications are shared between the student and employer assigned to that specific task.

You control visibility of certain information through your privacy settings, but core profile information necessary for platform functionality cannot be hidden.

4.2 With Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, including:

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Payment processors
  • Email service providers
  • Analytics services
  • Customer support platforms
  • Background check providers (with your consent)

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

4.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to:

  • Valid legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Government or regulatory requests
  • Requests from law enforcement
  • Protection of our rights, property, or safety
  • Emergency situations involving danger to persons

4.4 Business Transfers

If KADAM is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4.5 With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you explicitly consent to such sharing.

4.6 Verification Data Handling

Sensitive Verification Documents: ID cards, guardian approval letters, company registration documents, and other verification materials are:

  • Accessed only by authorized verification team members
  • Stored securely with encryption
  • Used solely for verification purposes
  • Not shared with other users or third parties (except as required by law)
  • Retained for compliance and audit purposes as specified in our Data Retention policy

4.7 Payment Information

Payment data (transaction amounts, advance and final payments) is:

  • Processed through secure payment service providers
  • Shared with payment processors only to complete transactions
  • Stored in encrypted format
  • Accessible to admin for dispute resolution and accounting
  • Subject to financial regulatory compliance requirements

Note: We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement various measures to protect it:

5.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure socket layer technology for payment processing
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Firewall protection and intrusion detection systems
  • Secure data centers with physical security measures

5.2 Organizational Safeguards

  • Access controls limiting who can view your information
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy
  • Confidentiality agreements with employees and contractors
  • Regular security policy reviews and updates

5.3 Your Responsibility

While we implement strong security measures, you also play a role in protecting your information:

  • Use a strong, unique password
  • Enable two-factor authentication if available
  • Keep your login credentials confidential
  • Log out after each session on shared devices
  • Report any suspicious activity immediately

Security Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by applicable law, typically within 72 hours of discovery.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6.1 Account Information

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you close your account, we will delete or anonymize your information within 90 days, except where we need to retain it for:

  • Legal obligations (tax, accounting, anti-fraud purposes)
  • Dispute resolution and task-related conflicts
  • Enforcement of our agreements and Terms and Conditions
  • Payment records and financial compliance
  • Business records and audit trails

6.2 Verification Documents

Verification documents (ID cards, approval letters, company registration documents) are retained for:

  • During active account: Entire duration of account activity
  • After account closure: Minimum 5 years for legal compliance and audit purposes
  • For users under 18: Guardian approval documents retained until student turns 21 or 3 years after account closure, whichever is longer

6.3 Task and Transaction Records

Task details, agreements, deliverables, and payment records are retained for:

  • Active tasks: Until task completion and payment closure
  • Completed tasks: 7 years for financial and tax compliance
  • Disputed tasks: Until resolution plus 3 years
  • Digital agreements: 7 years as legally binding contracts

6.4 Communications and Interactions

Messages, ratings, and communications on the platform are retained for:

  • Platform messages: 3 years after your last activity
  • Support tickets: 5 years for customer service improvement
  • Ratings and reviews: Permanently (unless deleted by mutual agreement or admin intervention)
  • Dispute evidence: 5 years after resolution

6.5 Experience Points and Levels

Your experience points, level history, and achievement data are retained permanently as they reflect your platform reputation and history. Upon account deletion, this data may be anonymized but retained for platform statistics.

6.6 Analytics Data

Aggregated and anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely for research and service improvement purposes.

7. Your Rights

7.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to access your personal information and request a copy in a structured, commonly used format. You can download your data from your account settings.

7.2 Correction

You have the right to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information. You can update most information directly in your account settings.

7.3 Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions. You can delete your account through your account settings.

7.4 Objection and Restriction

You can object to certain processing of your information or request that we restrict processing in specific circumstances.

7.5 Withdraw Consent

Where we process your information based on consent, you can withdraw that consent at any time. This will not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

7.6 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of marketing emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any marketing email or adjusting your communication preferences in your account settings.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@kadam.com or through your account settings. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our platform.

8.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us recognize you and remember your preferences. We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain until deleted or expired).

8.2 Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Required for the platform to function properly (authentication, security)

Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our Services

Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings

Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness

8.3 Other Tracking Technologies

  • Web beacons and pixel tags
  • Local storage (HTML5)
  • Mobile device identifiers
  • Analytics tools (Google Analytics, etc.)

8.4 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our Services. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Clear all cookies when closing the browser

10. Children's Privacy

KADAM allows students under the age of 18 to use the platform with proper parental/guardian consent and supervision, as students often include high school and college individuals who wish to earn money and build portfolios.

10.1 Requirements for Users Under 18

If you are under 18 years of age, you may use KADAM only if you provide:

  • Written guardian approval letter with signature
  • Guardian's contact information (phone number and email)
  • Valid ID card for age verification
  • Guardian consent for data processing

10.2 Parental/Guardian Rights

If you are a parent or guardian of a user under 18:

  • You have the right to access your child's account information
  • You can request deletion of your child's account
  • You will be notified of significant account activities
  • You can update or revoke consent at any time
  • We may contact you to verify tasks or resolve issues

10.3 Additional Protections

For users under 18, we implement additional safety measures:

  • Enhanced verification of employers before task assignment
  • Monitoring of task content for age-appropriateness
  • Restrictions on certain task categories that may not be suitable
  • Priority support for any safety concerns

10.4 Contact for Child Safety Concerns

If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns about your child's use of KADAM or believe your child has provided information without consent, please contact us immediately at childsafety@kadam.com or privacy@kadam.com.
