5A. Payment Terms & Platform Fees

5A.1 Platform Fee Structure

KADAM charges a 9.5% platform fee on the total task offer amount, deducted from the Employer's payment. This fee is used to maintain and improve the platform.

Calculation: Student's Income = Your Offer Amount - Platform Fee (9.5%)

5A.2 Advance Payment Requirement

Upon mutual acceptance of a task:

  • A digital agreement must be signed by both parties
  • Employer must pay a 10% advance payment to the Student before work commences
  • The advance payment demonstrates commitment and covers initial work costs

5A.3 Final Payment

Upon task completion and delivery:

  • Student submits completed work through the platform (supports image, video, audio, archives up to 1GB per file)
  • Employer reviews the deliverables
  • Employer releases the remaining 90% payment to the Student
  • Student marks payment as received
  • Task is closed and archived

5A.4 Minimum Payment

The minimum payment is automatically calculated based on estimated time required: Rs. 100 per hour minimum.

5A.5 Payment Disputes

In case of payment disputes, users may contact KADAM support. We reserve the right to mediate disputes and make final decisions regarding payment release or refunds.
